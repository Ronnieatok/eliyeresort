A Hidden Oasis on the Jade Sea

Nestled on Lake Turkana's shores (the "Jade Sea"), our eco-resort blends luxury with authentic Turkana culture through sustainable design and local craftsmanship.

🏡 Your Stay

  • Thatched bandas with lake views
  • Solar-powered comforts
  • Handcrafted local décor

🌿 Experiences

  • Kayaking & fishing on the lake
  • Cultural workshops with Turkana elders
  • Fossil exploration hikes
  • Stargazing in pristine dark skies
